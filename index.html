Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enable two-factor authentication?

To enable 2FA, go to your Security Settings and click on "Two-Factor Authentication". Follow the instructions to set it up using an authenticator app like Google Authenticator or Authy.

What are the trading fees on NexusPro?

Our fee structure is based on a maker-taker model. Makers pay 0.10% and takers pay 0.20%. Fees decrease based on your 30-day trading volume and NEXUS token holdings.

How long do withdrawals take?

Withdrawal processing times vary by cryptocurrency. Most withdrawals are processed within 30 minutes, but can take longer during periods of network congestion.
